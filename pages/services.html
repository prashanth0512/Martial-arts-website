We offer classes for children aged 5 and up, teens, and adults of all ages. Our children's programmes focus on discipline, coordination, and fun, while our adult classes offer fitness, self-defence, and competitive pathways. There's no upper age limit — many of our students start training in their 40s and 50s.

We recommend starting with 2–3 sessions per week. This gives your body enough time to recover while building skills consistently. As you progress, many students increase to 4–5 sessions per week. Our flexible memberships support whatever schedule works best for you.

Beginner and Intermediate plans include your starter gear (uniform or hand wraps depending on discipline). As you advance, you may wish to purchase your own personalised equipment. We have a pro shop on-site and can recommend the best gear for your level and goals.
